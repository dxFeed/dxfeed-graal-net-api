AuthOrderSource

Represents information about available symbols to the client for the entitled collection of OrderSource.

PriceLevelChecker

Utility class to check consistency of Order list in terms that in each time bid price is less than ask price. There is additional information may be gathered during the check:

  • Gap detection for subsequent events. In other words if there was a pause between orders greater than timeGapBound
  • Last bid and ask change with the minimal period of 1 second
  • Spike in quotes

Important to mention that sometimes it's a valid situation when bid greater or equal to ask.
PriceLevelService

A service that allows to subscribe to orders and quotes at a specified granularity.
